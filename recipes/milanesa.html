Milanesa

Description about the recipe

Milanesa or also called Milanesa Steak in english, it's a typicall dish in Argentina and Uruguay, it consists on a slice of meat cover with bread crumbs and deep fried on vegetable oil.

Ingredients

  • One slice of any meat you want (without bone)
  • One or two eggs
  • Bread crumbs
  • Salt and any other seasoning you like
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. To start with the meat milanesa recipe, you must first season the meat with salt, pepper and oregano to taste. Then, pass each of the veal fillets through the breadcrumbs you need (on both sides).
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs and bathe the steaks in them. Then, to fix the breading of the milanesas, pass them through the breadcrumbs again.
  3. Now, put the oil to heat and, when it is hot, fry the meat fillets in batter over medium heat. You should fry them until they are golden brown on both sides.
  4. To remove excess oil from the meat milanesas you can place them on absorbent paper. Ready to eat!
